Alex Pereira Accuses Ciryl Gane of Illegal Strikes After UFC White House Defeat

Published June 2026 • Stylec Sport

The fallout from UFC Freedom 250 continues as Alex Pereira claims illegal strikes played a role in his defeat against Ciryl Gane during their heavyweight showdown.

A Historic Opportunity Ends in Defeat

Alex Pereira entered UFC Freedom 250 with the chance to make history. The former middleweight and light heavyweight champion moved up to heavyweight hoping to become the first fighter in UFC history to win championships across three divisions.

Standing across from him was Ciryl Gane, one of the most technical heavyweights in the sport.

However, Gane delivered one of the biggest performances of his career, controlling the distance and eventually securing a second-round stoppage victory.

Pereira's Main Complaint

After the fight, Pereira claimed that several strikes landed illegally near the back of his head during the finishing sequence.

The Brazilian star believes those strikes affected his ability to recover and questioned whether the result should stand.

The controversy centers around MMA rules that prohibit strikes to certain areas of the back of the head because of the increased risk of serious injury.

Criticism of Herb Dean

Pereira's frustration was not only directed toward Ciryl Gane. He also criticized referee Herb Dean for allowing the sequence to continue.

Dean defended his decision, explaining that judging illegal strikes during fast-moving exchanges is extremely difficult and that referees must make immediate decisions inside the Octagon.

Possible Appeal

Pereira and his team have discussed the possibility of appealing the result.

However, overturning MMA results is extremely rare unless there is clear evidence of a major officiating error or rule violation.

The Other Side of the Debate

Many fans believe Gane was simply the better fighter on the night.

They argue that Pereira was already struggling before the finishing sequence, with Gane controlling the pace using movement, speed, and his jab.

What Happens Next?

For Pereira, the immediate priority is recovery after suffering a major setback and receiving a six-month medical suspension.

The former champion must decide whether he will continue his heavyweight journey or return to light heavyweight.

Final Thoughts

The Alex Pereira vs Ciryl Gane controversy has become one of the biggest talking points following UFC Freedom 250.

Pereira believes illegal strikes changed the outcome. Critics believe Gane earned the victory.

Regardless of the debate, the official result remains unchanged and Pereira now begins the road back toward another championship opportunity.
