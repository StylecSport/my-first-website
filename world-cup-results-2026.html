Germany Focus: A Statement Start

Germany entered the tournament under pressure to prove they remain one of the world’s elite football nations.

The 7-1 victory over Curacao was more than a scoreline. It showed confidence, movement, attacking organisation, and a team playing with freedom.

Their midfield controlled the rhythm of the match while their forwards constantly created problems.
