Canelo Targets Christian Mbilli After Crawford Retirement

Published May 27, 2026 • Stylec Sport

Saul "Canelo" Alvarez is preparing for a major return as he targets a WBC super middleweight title clash against Christian Mbilli.

🔥 The Crawford Situation

The Mexican superstar has reportedly grown frustrated after a potential rematch with Terence Crawford collapsed following Crawford’s retirement decision.

Crawford, who previously defeated Canelo in a historic clash, has stepped away from boxing citing health preservation and a desire to end his career at the top.

🏆 WBC Title Controversy

Reports suggest Crawford’s refusal to comply with updated WBC sanctioning fees contributed to him being stripped of his title status.

⚔️ New Challenger: Christian Mbilli

Attention now shifts to undefeated contender Christian Mbilli, known for his aggressive pressure and explosive combinations.

📊 Fight Analysis

Analysts believe this matchup presents a very different challenge for Canelo compared to elite counter-punchers like Crawford.

If confirmed, this fight could define the next phase of the super middleweight division.
